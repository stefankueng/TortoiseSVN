Missing Languages

Would you like to translate TortoiseSVN into your native language?
Read these instructions first.

  • If if you want to translate the current development version, download an empty catalog (development). Translate what you can and send us your translation.
  • If you want to translate the last official release (TortoiseSVN 1.1.2), download an empty catalog (release) and start to translate. You can even test your translations in real life. To do so, you have to download the source dlls for TortoiseSVN 1.1.1 and the ResText Utility to insert your translations into the dlls.

When you are finished, send us your translation (just the .po file) to patches@tortoisesvn.tigris.org. We will be glad to include it in the next TortoiseSVN release.
